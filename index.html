About Me

I’m Manikanta Addala, a dedicated Sustainability Officer passionate about driving impactful environmental initiatives. With a solid foundation in Agriculture and Sustainability, I’ve had the privilege of working across diverse sectors, implementing strategic sustainability solutions and fostering eco-efficiency within organizations.

Holding a Master of Sustainability from Deakin University and a Bachelor of Science in Agriculture from Lovely Professional University, I am deeply committed to blending scientific insights with sustainable practices. My academic journey, enriched with hands-on experience, has equipped me with the ability to analyze complex environmental data, assess sustainability challenges, and create actionable, results-driven solutions.

Professional Experience

As a Sustainability Intern at Advantageous Systems LLC I developed plans for pollution prevention by working with industrial facilities to design waste treatment systems or recycling programs. Facilitated studies to identify potential environmental impacts of proposed development projects. Assessed, sort, characterized and pack known and unknown materials. Prepared, maintained, and revised quality assurance documentation and procedures.

During my time as a Sustainability Coordinator at Kalgudi Digital Pvt Ltd I Conducted research and analysis of energy, water, waste, and air emissions data to inform sustainability initiatives. Facilitated studies to identify potential environmental impacts of proposed development projects. Wrote reports about environmental concerns, making recommendations for improving conditions.

As an Environmental Consultant at Millet Break I Advised clients on effective methods for monitoring air quality levels within facilities. Evaluated effectiveness of existing environmental management systems. Designed remediation plans for contaminated sites based on risk assessment results.
